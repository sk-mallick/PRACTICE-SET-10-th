Q1. Student: "Hello. I've come to ask about the English courses you run for
    international students."
    Administrator: "Are you a student at the university?"
    The student greeted and mentioned that she _______ to inquire about the
    English courses for international students. The administrator enquired
    if she was a student at the university.                            (2025)

Answer: had come
Solution: In reported speech, 'have come' (Present Perfect) shifts to 'had
          come' (Past Perfect). 'She' replaces 'I' (first person to third).
